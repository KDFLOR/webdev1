Media

The disadvantaged or working class is mostly associated with negative stereotypes and representations in the media. Middle and upper classes, however, receive much more positive representation and their lifestyles are glorified.

Employment

Resume audit studies done where it looks for social class background cues, and they’ve found that having cues of an upper class background advandges you for entry level and managerial positions these cues include hobbies seemgly like you played rugby or lacrosse advanges you rather than from playing basketball or soccer

A lot of companies engage in network based recruitment forms of hiring so they will ask their current employees for referrals assuming that they will refer people who are in the same social class

Health

Those with the lowest socioeconomic status are two to three times as likely to suffer from a mental health disorder than those with the highest socioeconomic status.
