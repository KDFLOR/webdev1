    • Short-Term Benefits of Meditation

    Researchers have found that even short-term mindfulness meditative practice of about 15 minutes can help you make wiser choices when making decisions. You build awareness of the present moment and try to let go of other thoughtd that distract.

    Long-Term Benefits of Meditation

    Well being is not a sprint, but a marathon. Healthy habits take time to form, therefore long term meditation over some time forms benefits such as increase attention span and concentration, decreased anxiety, reduced depression, increases gray and white matter in the brain,increases gamma brain waves, improves sleep, speeds stress recovery, calms the mind, enhances creativity, improves memory etc
